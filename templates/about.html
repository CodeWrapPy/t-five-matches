The story behind Harichand Totaldas and the birth of T-Five Matches.

A Legacy Built on Quality & Trust

Founded by Harichand Totaldas, our company began as a small family workshop dedicated to the craft of matchstick making. Over three generations, that modest operation has evolved into a full-scale manufacturing powerhouse.

Today, T-Five Matches is synonymous with reliability. We specialize in custom-branded matchbox manufacturing, helping businesses worldwide illuminate their own brand identity with precision and care.
